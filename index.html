China extends technical hold on Azhar’s terror listing at UN

Friday,4-August-2017

China has again extended by three months its technical hold on the US, France and UK- backed proposal to designate Pakistan-based JeM terror group chief Masood Azhar as a global terrorist by the UN.
China had in February this year blocked the U.S. move to designate Azhar, the Pathankot terror attack mastermind, as a global terrorist at the U.N.
